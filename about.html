About Us

The Cleanic is your premier choice for professional cleaning services across New Zealand. Established with a passion for cleanliness and customer satisfaction, we have grown into a trusted name in the industry, serving homes, businesses, and Airbnb properties with unparalleled dedication. Our mission is to transform your space with excellence, care, and eco-friendly practices, ensuring every corner shines with pride.

Our Mission

At The Cleanic, our mission is to provide top-tier cleaning solutions that enhance the quality of life for our clients. We aim to create a healthier, happier environment by using sustainable cleaning products and innovative techniques. Our commitment extends beyond just cleaning—we strive to build lasting relationships with our clients, offering personalized services tailored to their unique needs. Whether it's a cozy home in Auckland or a bustling office in Wellington, we bring the same level of expertise and enthusiasm to every job.

Our History

Founded in 2018, The Cleanic began as a small family-run business with a single goal: to offer exceptional cleaning services to the New Zealand community. Starting with a handful of dedicated cleaners, we quickly gained a reputation for reliability and quality. Over the years, we expanded our team and services, incorporating advanced cleaning technologies and eco-friendly practices. Today, we proudly serve clients nationwide, with a strong presence in major cities like Auckland, Christchurch, and Hamilton, and we continue to grow with the support of our loyal customers.
