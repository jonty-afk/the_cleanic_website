End of Tenancy Cleaning

Our End of Tenancy Cleaning service is the perfect solution to prepare your rental property for a seamless move-out process, ensuring it meets the highest standards required for inspections and new tenants. This exhaustive cleaning service is crafted with precision to address every aspect of your space, leaving it in an immaculate condition that guarantees your deposit return and impresses property managers. Our dedicated team employs advanced cleaning methods and top-tier products to perform a full kitchen deep clean, meticulously scrubbing ovens, refrigerators, and stovetops to eliminate grease, food residues, and lingering odors. Bathroom sterilization is conducted with rigorous attention, disinfecting every surface including toilets, sinks, showers, and tiles to eradicate bacteria and maintain a spotless hygiene level. Floor polishing and stain removal transform hard surfaces and carpets, removing scuffs, spills, and wear marks to restore their original beauty. Wall spot cleaning tackles smudges and marks, while window and blind cleaning ensures crystal-clear views and dust-free interiors. This service is priced between $200 and $400, varying with property size, and comes with a robust 48-hour guarantee to address any concerns, providing you with peace of mind during a stressful transition. Whether you’re a landlord or tenant, our End of Tenancy Cleaning is your key to a hassle-free handover, executed with professionalism and care every step of the way.
