One-Off Cleaning & Water Blasting

Our One-Off Cleaning & Water Blasting service offers a powerful, all-in-one solution to tackle deep-seated dirt and grime in your home or outdoor spaces, providing a thorough clean for those occasional or special needs with exceptional results. This versatile service combines interior deep cleaning with high-pressure water blasting to address a wide range of cleaning challenges, making it ideal for pre-move-in preparation, post-event restoration, or seasonal property maintenance. Inside, our expert team performs a detailed clean of all rooms, scrubbing walls, floors, and surfaces to remove stubborn stains, dust, and buildup, using eco-friendly products that ensure safety for your family and pets. Outdoors, our water blasting technique employs adjustable pressure settings to blast away mold, algae, and dirt from driveways, patios, decks, and siding, revitalizing your exterior with a fresh, clean look without damaging delicate surfaces. Furniture and appliance cleaning is included, with special attention to hard-to-reach areas, while a final polish enhances the overall shine and hygiene. Priced between $120 and $300 depending on the scope and area size, this service is a cost-effective way to achieve a spotless environment, offering customizable packages to suit your specific requirements and leaving your property looking renewed and pristine.
